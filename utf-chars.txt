to make them more vivid 👍, visible 💥 and even humorous 🐵, wow!

The UTF group titles are arbitrary while the characters are sorted by their codes - not by their sense.

This file is best viewed in alited editor available in the links:

  https://chiselapp.com/user/aplsimple/repository/alited/download
  https://github.com/aplsimple/alited
